First step

It's always better to take your step. Our journey with first step begins with "Toycathon" world's biggest hackathon for toys/games. This is when our journey of long learning and success begins as we "Kodo Shinobis" succeeded to be the finalist with no prior experience of such a huge competition. We built a game named "Element Hunt"(some works yet to be done), which is basically a digital game which will help you to learn the complex periodic table of chemistry in an easy and fun way. We encourage you to take your "first step" and be our guest if you need any help. Do visit our contact us page for a healthy interaction.

Consistency is the key

Yes! We all are familiar with the aforesaid phrase. And therefore we have continued to learn new skills and keep working on new projects. And the result was that we build a mind exercise game "Brain teaser" which so interactive and competitive that player will love to give it a shot once.

It was a bit challenge for us as we this was our first project in "Android Studio" for a mobile application. But with our knowledge and belief in ourselves and with determination to provide a world with better learning platform we were able to do it within a span of three weeks.

During this project we came to learn about different things like- "How to manage Admob account of Google Adsense", "How to open a playstore account and publish your application ". If you are interested to learn Android development, to manage ads in your website/application, to open and understand different aspects of google playstore, then contact us. We will be happy to help you.
